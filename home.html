Unchained Melody

Owned by the families of composer Alex North and lyricist Hy Zaret, Unchained Melody Publishing LLC is the publishing administrator for 100% of the copyright in "Unchained Melody."

We are committed to providing immediate responses to all licensing inquiries, and we are willing to review a project's overall budget when formulating a quote.

To facilitate one-stop, one phone call deals, we also control a number of "Unchained Melody" re-records in various styles. We are able to make extremely favorable all-in deals with these masters.
